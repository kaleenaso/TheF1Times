SEASON RECAP • 2021

22 Races for 2021

Two inaugural Grand Prix, several circuit tweaks, a few crashes but a whole lot of racing as the dramatic, exciting 2021 season comes to an end with Max Verstappen crowned with his first Formula One Driver’s Championship title as well as the first Dutch to do so. Mercedes’ Lewis Hamilton just misses coming out in second in the driver’s championship after the final lap at the Abu Dhabi Grand Prix yet his team achieves their 8th consecutive Constructors Championship title.

We travel across 4 continents returning to the countries in the west as we look back at some highlights with the 22-race season.
