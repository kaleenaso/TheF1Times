SEASON RECAP • 2021

22 Races for 2021

Formula 1 is back for 2021 everyone. With 3 new drivers, 4 former champions, 10 Grand Prix winners, the most races in a season, and maybe a history-making record-breaking 8 Championship title for Lewis Hamitlon. We enter the year for one history making season and after 10 months here are some of the season highlights.

Watch some highlights of the 2021 season below:

From Bahrain to Abu Dhabi, across 4 continents, two inaugural Grand Prix, several circuit tweaks, a few crashes but a whole lot of racing as the dramatic, exciting 2021 season comes to an end with Max Verstappen crowned with his first Formula One Driver’s Championship title as well as the first Dutch to do so. Mercedes’ Lewis Hamilton just misses coming out in second in the driver’s championship after the final lap at the Abu Dhabi Grand Prix yet his team achieves the 8th consecutive Constructors Championship title.

Returning to Monaco and countries in the west as we look back at some notable moments with the 22 Grand Prix this season.
